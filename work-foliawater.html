Folia Water | Product Research


Supervisors: Teri Dankovich, Cantwell Carson // Duration: Aug 2016 - Dec 2016
Contribution: Microbiological Research, Experiment Design, Protocol Development and Writing

"How effective are Folia Filters at removing particulate matter from unsafe drinking water?"

During my sophomore fall semester, I worked in Folia Water's science department. I focused on microbiological testing of the Folia Filters product, a silver-based filter paper that serves as a cheap water filtration solution for those in need.

Product Testing and Research

My role as microbiological lab associate involved growing bacterial cultures of non-pathogenic E. coli, treating water with said cultures to create sample 'infected' influent water, and then running the 'infected' water through the Folia Filters. The effluent water collected through the filter were then analyzed for further bacterial growth, to give an indication of the percentage of surviving bacteria post-filtration.


Lab Management

As the primary lab associate, I managed the usage of scientific tools and equipment. Any new projects or protocols were developed and recorded by myself, and then shared with Folia Water for future usage and improvement.
