UberPool | UX Research


Supervisor: Kaylee White // Duration: Nov 2018 - Dec 2018
Skills: Stakeholder Mapping, User Interviews, Affinity Diagramming, Primary and Secondary Research

"How can we improve the CMU graduate student experience with public transportation?"

For our core course in User-Centered Research and Evaluation, our team was tasked to investigate the current state of public transportation for graduate students at CMU. We used a variety of user research methods, including but not limited to contextual interviews, creating sequence and day-in-the-life models, and affinity diagramming, to better understand the existing pain points and possible areas of improvement in this problem space. After our 2 month long investigation, we presented our stakeholders with one possible solution, UberPool, with a poster and research brief.
