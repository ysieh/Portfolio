AEO Express


Duration: Oct 2018 - Dec 2018
Role: Service Designer and Research
Course, professor: Service Design

The problem space: "What can American Eagle learn from its employees and shoppers to innovate and improve their existing services?"

For the class Service Design, we worked directly with American Eagle Outfitters (AEO) to investigate service opportunities within American Eagle and its constituent brants. Our team of 5 individuals worked for half a semester exploring different stakeholders in American Eagle's current services, and eventually proposed the concept of AEO Express.
